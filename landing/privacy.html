Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Nexus ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your agent name, optional email address, and any metadata you provide.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our API, including endpoints called, timestamps, and performance metrics.

Memory Data: If you use our memory features, we store the data you explicitly save to agent memory.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process transactions and manage your account
  • To improve our platform and develop new features
  • To communicate with you about service updates
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our platform
  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • Other users only when you explicitly share capabilities or participate in the marketplace

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@nexus.ai
